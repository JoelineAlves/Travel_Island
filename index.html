Snorkelling with Turtle

This fantastic experience aims to keep people in direct contact with our marine fauna, through diving. In addition to turtles, you can also have contact with other species such as Mantas, Rays and many species of colorful fish, even endemic.

The famous Carnival

The carnival experience is fantastic to see, so many local people together with hundreds of dancers, exotic costumes and huge floats, not to mention the samba and singers.

Coastal beaches in the north of Baía

Praia do Norte or Norte da Baía is a beach located between Baía das Gatas and Praia Grande. These are areas used for water sports such as surfing and body boarding.

Monte Cara

Monte Cara is truly the symbol of the island of São Vicente. It is one of the best-known elevations on the island of S. Vicente, which owes its name to the fact that its outline resembles a human face looking at the sky.

The Best Activities

Cachupa guisado

When the topic of conversation is Cape Verdean food, the first word that comes to mind is Cachupa, the most popular traditional dish in Cape Verde.

Fateja beach

Praia da Fateja is located behind Monte Cara. It is mostly rocky, a beautiful beach, a magnificent place, with natural beauties that provide a relaxed landscape and a natural pool for a dip after the walk

"12 Turns"

“12 turns” is a sandy underground labyrinth, sculpted by water at the foot of Monte Cara, in the Lazareto area.

Jet ski ride

Explore the excitement of jet-skiing across the stunning Porto Grande Bay. You'll have the chance to experience the speed and adrenaline while riding a jet-ski from Marina do Mindelo to the breathtaking beach of Laginha during this thrilling trip.

San Jon

The feast of São João Baptista, better known as “San Jon”. It is one of the main popular festivities in the islands of Cape Verde To the sound of drums, whistles and voices, people dance and celebrate a party where the sacred mixes with the profane in a harmonious and vibrant way.
